Average Distance Traveled

I calculated average distanced traveled by estimating that the average person rides a bike at a pace of 15km/hr and then converting that into 4.2 m/s and multiplying it by the duration of a ride and finding the mean of all trips

Communter Data:

I estimated that the number of communters was around 494 people. I got this number by taking the data of the number of trips everyday. If a person was communting to work they will most likely do this on a daily basis. Thus, the average number of trips will give you the closest number to the number of communters since the average gives you the average trips done everyday and a communter would on average do one trip everyday because they would have to go to work daily.

Season Data:

I graphed all the trips on a bar graph with the y axis being the number of trips done in that specific month. By doing this, I discovered that late summer, early fall (08-10) were the most popular season with a peak on August. After August, there is a strong decrease in trips for the winter season (12 - 03).
